About Me

I am an undergraduated information system student of Tanjungpura University. I am someone who is enthusiastic about Front-End and also Back-End. I also have a passion for UI/UX Designer. In my academic journey, I have always been interested in website programming on the Front-End and Back-End, I also prioritize achieving excellent results and the process of achieving those results.
